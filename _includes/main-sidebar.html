My name's Eduardo and I'm a digital media researcher and designer. I'm currently working on digital innovation for La Victoria Lab, a human-centered design lab part of the Intercorp Group in Lima, Peru.

In 2014, I graduated from the Comparative Media Studies master's program at MIT, where I did research on the rise of indie video game development in Peru and its potential as creative industry. I was also research assistant at The Education Arcade, an MIT lab doing research and development of educational games.
